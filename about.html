About Us

Hi I'm Ethan Chen, the owner and main developer here at Queensboro Hill Web Design. I started learning how to develop websites while studying at Queens College and graduated with a Bachelors of Science in Computer Science in 2022. Since then I've worked as a Software Engineer but my main passion has always been building websites.

I've seen local businesses in my area struggle to create an online presence and I want to help in the way I know best, which is building modern websites that are highly performant and search engine optimized. My goal is to take on the hassle of building your website so you can focus on the business decisions that matter.
